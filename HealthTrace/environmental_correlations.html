Key Environmental Health Insights

🫁 Respiratory Diseases (Influenza)
PM2.5 levels above 25 μg/m³ show 82% correlation with increased influenza cases. Winter months with poor air quality create optimal conditions for respiratory infections.
💧 Water-Related Diseases (Legionellosis)
Water temperatures above 25°C combined with high humidity (>70%) create ideal conditions for Legionella growth, with 71% correlation to case increases.
🍽️ Foodborne Diseases (Hepatitis A)
E.coli levels above 100 CFU/100ml indicate water contamination with 85% correlation to Hepatitis A outbreaks, especially after heavy rainfall periods.
📊 Predictive Modeling
GAM, DLNM, and Random Forest models show high accuracy in predicting disease outbreaks based on environmental data, enabling early warning systems.
